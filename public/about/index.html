About

Duaity Link to heading

Duality is an independent research project developing SPECTRUM — a physics-informed framework for financial market dynamics, combining non-equilibrium thermodynamics, differential geometry (Einstein-Cartan), and entropy dynamics. Founded by Jeffee Hsiung, a KU Leuven graduate with research background at IMEC in radar 3D imaging and few-shot deep learning. Currently focused on bridging physical principles with financial modeling.

🔬 Research Philosophy Link to heading

Duality believes that financial markets, despite their apparent randomness, exhibit underlying geometric and spectral structures that can be uncovered through rigorous mathematical analysis. The current work focuses on:

  • 📊 Spectral methods (Fourier, Laplace, wavelet transforms) for market cycle identification
  • 🧮 Geometry manifolds learning for asset behavior classification
  • ⚙️ Building robust, automated trading systems that execute strategies with discipline
